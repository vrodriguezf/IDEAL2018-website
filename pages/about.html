Visa

If you require a Letter of Invitation to attend this event, contact with Organizers of the conference (ideal2018@uam.es).
Letters of Invitation are usually only required by foreigners who must obtain a VISA in order to attend the conference. All persons who require a Letter of Invitation to attend the conference must be registered and paid in full on or before Jul 31, 2018. Letters of Invitation will only be issued to the registrant and only after the registration is paid in full. The letter of invitation issued by IDEAL 2018 is not a commitment to provide any financial support nor does it guarantee a visa.
