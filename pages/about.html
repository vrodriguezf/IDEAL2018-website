Visa

If you require a Letter of Invitation to attend this event, contact with Organizers of the conference (ideal2018@uam.es).
Letters of Invitation are usually only required by foreigners who must obtain a VISA in order to attend the conference. All persons who require a Letter of Invitation to attend the conference must be registered and paid in full on or before Jul 31, 2018. Letters of Invitation will only be issued to the registrant and only after the registration is paid in full. The letter of invitation issued by IDEAL 2018 is not a commitment to provide any financial support nor does it guarantee a visa.

Local Attractions

Royal Palace

The largest royal palace in Western Europe is a must-visit for all.

Puerta del Sol

Puerta del Sol, the symbolic centre of Madrid, where the city’s main streets meet.

Plaza Mayor

This arcaded square located in the historical centre of the city is the heart of Hapsburg Madrid.

Paseo del Arte

A one-kilometre stretch that is home to three of the best museums in the world: the Prado, the Thyssen-Bornemisza and the Reina Sofía.

Cibeles Fountain

Marking the start of the Art Walk, this fountain is one of Madrid's icons.

The Rastro

Spain's best known street market is one of the oldest in Madrid, dating back to mediaeval times.

Matadero Madrid

The city's old slaughterhouse, at Madrid Rio, has been transformed into a cultural megacomplex.
