Local Attractions

IDEAL 2018 takes place in Madrid, one of the most fascinating destinations in Europe! The capital of Spain, located in the heart of the Iberian Peninsula and right in the center of the Castillian plain, because of its central location and high altitude, the climate of Madrid is characterized by warm dry summers and cool winters. This cosmopolitan city, is the headquarters for the Public Administration, Government, Spanish Parliament and the home of the Spanish Royal Family. The city is an excellent combination of modern infrastructure and historic squares, churches, monuments and city gates which make this one of Europe’s most attractive cities for sight-seeing. Please find below some of the most remarkable tourist attractions in Madrid.

Royal Palace

The largest royal palace in Western Europe is a must-visit for all.

Puerta del Sol

Puerta del Sol, the symbolic centre of Madrid, where the city’s main streets meet.

Plaza Mayor

This arcaded square located in the historical centre of the city is the heart of Hapsburg Madrid.

Paseo del Arte

A one-kilometre stretch that is home to three of the best museums in the world: the Prado, the Thyssen-Bornemisza and the Reina Sofía.

Cibeles Fountain

Marking the start of the Art Walk, this fountain is one of Madrid's icons.

The Rastro

Spain's best known street market is one of the oldest in Madrid, dating back to mediaeval times.

Matadero Madrid

The city's old slaughterhouse, at Madrid Rio, has been transformed into a cultural megacomplex.
