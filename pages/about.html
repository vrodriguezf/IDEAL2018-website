  • tripadvisor.com
  • booking.com
  • trivago.com
We highly recommed to stay in Hotel Siete Islas, in the centre of Madrid, which has a Special 15% Discount for Early Booking Offer. Accommodation booking and payment has to be made to the hotel directly. Please find below the information related to this hotel:
  • Hotel Siete Islas (approximately 80 euros Standard single room, 90 euros Standard double room) (Breakfast included).
Other suggestions for places to stay with convenient access to the university campus are the following:

The conference venue is approximately 20 km. north to the city center, so the most convenient way to stay within Madrid well connected via subway to enjoy the free time in the city and at the same time not spending too much time every day to attend the conference is staying in the north of the city. The best areas in this sense are close to Chamartin (commuter train station). We recommend staying as close as possible to Chamartin station, as getting to the campus using the commuter train is very fast (line C-4, only two stops to reach Cantoblanco Universidad, about 15 minutes + 10 minutes walk from Cantoblanco Universidad station to the venue). Right there in Chamartin station we recommend:

Hotel Husa Chamartin (****)
