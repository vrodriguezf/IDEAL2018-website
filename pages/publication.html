Paper Submission

Authors are invited to submit their manuscripts (in pdf format) written in English by the deadline via the conference online submission system (accessible via IDEAL 2018 website). All submissions will be refereed by experts in the field based on originality, significance, quality and clarity. All contributions must be original, should not have been published elsewhere and must not be submitted elsewhere during the review period.

The nominal length of each paper is 8 pages and any paper that exceeds this limit will incur extra page charges on the registration form. In any cases, a paper must not exceed 12 LNCS pages. Papers must comply with the format of Springer LNCS/LNAI Proceedings. (see www.springer.com/lncs)

Submission to Workshop/Special Sessions using one of the following methods:

  • Please submit using the IDEAL 2018 submission link as above as to the main track but make note of the paper ID after the submission. Then email the paper ID and Special Session name to [ideal2018@uam.es]
  • Add the Workshop or Special Session name to the first line of the abstract when submitting using the IDEAL 2018 submission link.

Indexing

Accepted papers presented at the conference (main track, special sessions and workshops)will be included in the Proceedings of IDEAL 2018, to be published by Springer in its LNCS series, which is indexed in EI. In addition, selected papers will be invited for special issues in several leading international journals in the field, including the International Journal of Neural Systems (IJNS), Journal of Data Science and Analytics, etc.
