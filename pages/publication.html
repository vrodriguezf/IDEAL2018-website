Paper Submission

Authors are invited to submit their manuscripts (in pdf format) written in English by the deadline via the Easychair submission system (link is shown below). All submissions will be refereed by experts in the field based on originality, significance, quality and clarity. All contributions must be original, should not have been published elsewhere and must not be submitted elsewhere during the review period.

The nominal length of each paper is 8 pages and any paper that exceeds this limit will incur extra page charges on the registration form. In any cases, a paper must not exceed 12 LNCS pages. Papers must comply with the format of Springer LNCS/LNAI Proceedings. (see www.springer.com/lncs)

If you are interested in submitting a paper to a special session or a workshop instead of the regular session, you still need to submit the paper via the EasyChair submission system, and select the corresponding special session/workshop in the track list. With regard to the format and length of the papers, the general rules described above for regular submissions are applied, unless stated otherwise in the description of the corresponding special session/workshop. Final Accepted Papers

Accepted papers presented at the conference (main track, special sessions and workshops) will be included in the Proceedings of IDEAL 2018, to be published by Springer in its LNCS series, which is indexed in EI.

Final versions of the accepted papers must be uploaded via Easychair. Papers must comply the format of LNCS/LNAI conference proceedings (see these guidelines and templates).

You MUST register to the conference and pay the registration fee before uploading your final paper. Papers will be included in the proceedings only if at least one author has registered and paid the registration fee by Sep 8, 2018.

No paper will be published without the submitted copyright form. Once downloaded and filled, authors must include it during the submission of the final version of the paper.
